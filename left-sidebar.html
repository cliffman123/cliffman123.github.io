Summary

What is Cut the Cheese?

Cut the Cheese is a metroidvania plaformer style game. You play as a character, Blade to adventure through the lands of Draft City to find the lost seven ancient cheeses that was protected by family for generation. One rain day, a group of mouse found four of the seven lost cheese. If all seven cheese come together it can grant one wish. Blade has a feeling they are going to use it to summon their mouse overlord. There is only one cat that can stop their rain of terror and thats Blade. Blade has to Cut-the-Cheese.

How did this came to be?

While my friend, Anthony and I were studing in the libary we were making a joke of a story that would be funny to play. We decided to make a game out of it. My friend Anthony handle majority of the art. I and with the help of friend, Niall work on the coding aspect of the game.

What Programming Language is being used?

The game engine we used is GameMaker Studo. The engine has its own specal language GameMaker Language (GML). GML is similar to C++ and C.
